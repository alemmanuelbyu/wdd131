The Cozy Cup Site Plan

Site Name

The site name is: The Cozy Cup

This name represents a warm and inviting coffee shop where customers can relax and enjoy a cup of coffee. I chose this name because it evokes a sense of comfort and coziness, which is perfect for a coffee shop.

Optional domain availability: thecozycup.com

Site Purpose

The site provides information about The Cozy Cup coffee shop, including our menu, location, and hours of operation. The site will also allow customers to contact us and learn more about our shop.

Scenarios

  • What types of coffee drinks do you offer?
  • How can I get in touch with The Cozy Cup?

Color Schema

  • Beige (#F5F5DC) - Background color
  • Coffee brown (#964B00) - Headings
  • Green (#8BC34A) - Accents

Typography

  • Noto Serif - Headings
  • Open Sans - Body text
